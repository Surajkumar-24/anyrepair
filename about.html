About AnyRepair

AnyRepair was founded by Krishna and Suraj to solve a simple but widespread problem — laptop repairs in India are often opaque, inconvenient, and unreliable.


Customers are frequently left guessing about actual issues, fair pricing, and repair quality. We set out to change that by building a service focused on honest diagnostics, clear communication, and professional delivery.

What We Do

We provide doorstep laptop diagnosis and repair services, allowing customers to get their laptops checked and repaired at home. Our technicians identify the problem at the customer’s location, explain the issue clearly, and share a transparent price quote before any repair work begins.


Repairs are carried out only after customer approval.

Our Presence

Started in September 2025, and Today, we serve 30+ customers daily across Pune, Pimpri, and Chinchwad.


We deliberately operate within a limited geography to maintain consistent service quality and fast response times.

Our Vision

Our long-term vision is to expand AnyRepair into multiple technology repair and support categories, creating a dependable service platform for individuals and businesses alike.


At present, we remain focused exclusively on laptop repair services in Pune to ensure every customer receives reliable and professional support.
